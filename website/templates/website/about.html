ABOUT


Justice Art and Music on Stage (JAMS) is an annual event presented by PERFILMA FH UI that aims to raise awareness about current issues happening in our society through art and music.

With the tagline “Lead the Scene, Keep It Green”, JAMS 2021 is back to maintain a clean and habitable environment for generations to come.

Bringing "A Pledge to Our World" as our theme, JAMS 2021 is held to raise awareness about the arising environmental problems, specifically about deforestation, biodiversity loss, and waste as it is our responsibility to protect and heal our environment.
